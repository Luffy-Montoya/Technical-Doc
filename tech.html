The Straw Hat Pirates
  • Pictured from left to right:
  • Robin, Nami, Usopp, Zoro, 
  • Chopper, Sanji, Luffy

The Straw Hat Pirates are an extremely infamous and powerful pirate crew that originated from the East Blue. The "Straw Hats" are named after their captain Luffy's signature straw hat. Despite their small size, the Straw Hats are an extremely diverse group, possessing a wide variety of skills and coming from an array of backgrounds. While they all have different goals, ambitions, and dreams, they all share the same mission... to find the One Piece.

Monkey D. Luffy
  • Hometown: Foosha Village
  • Crew Role: Captain
  • Dream: To be the King of the Pirates

Luffy is the founder, captain, and strongest fighter of the Straw Hat Pirates. He fearlessly pursues the One Piece, the legendary treasure of the late Gol D. Roger, in order to become the new King of the Pirates and reach a further, untold dream. His greatest inspiration was the pirate "Red-Haired" Shanks, who sacrificed an arm to save his life, unknowingly enabled him to eat the Gum-Gum Devil Fruit that turned his body into rubber, and gave him his signature straw hat as a symbol of their vow to reunite when Luffy becomes a great pirate.

Roronoa Zoro
  • Hometown: Shimotsuki Village
  • Crew Role: Combatant
  • Dream: To be the world's greatest swordsman

Roronoa "Pirate Hunter" Zoro is the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates and is recognized as Luffy's right-hand man. Formerly a bounty hunter, he was the first person to join the Straw Hats. As a master of Three Sword Style, Zoro is one of the three most powerful combatants of the Straw Hats, alongside Luffy and Sanji. He joined Luffy after he saved Zoro from execution by the Marine. Zoro pursues his dream of becoming the greatest swordsman in the world to honor a promise he made to his deceased childhood friend Kuina.

Nami
  • Hometown: Cocoyashi Village
  • Crew Role: Navigator
  • Dream: To make a map of the world

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates and she was the second member to join to crew. As a child, she developed a love for drawing maps and hoped to one day draw a map of the entire world. A master thief, she initially "joined" the Straw Hats with the intention of robbing them so that she could buy the freedom of her village from the Arlong Pirates. She officially joined the crew after Luffy and the Straw Hats freed her village by defeating Arlong and his crew.

Usopp
  • Hometown: Syrup Village
  • Crew Role: Sniper
  • Dream: To be a great warrior of the sea

"Captain" Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates and was the third member to join the crew. A master of fake story-telling, Usopp commands an imaginary crew of over 8,000 men, which he always reminds his enemies of in a futile attempt to strike fear in their hearts. Usopp joined the Straw Hats after they saved his close friend Kaya and Syrup Village from the Black Cat Pirates. Despite his usual cowardice, Usopp dreams of becoming a brave warrior of the sea just like his father.

Sanji
  • Hometown: Germa Kingdom / Baratie
  • Crew Role: Chef / Cook
  • Dream: To find the All Blue

Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates and was the fourth member to join the crew. He met the Straw Hats at the Baratie where he was a cook and was doubling as a waiter the day that they met. It was his obsessive lust for Nami that intially drew him towards the Straw Hats, but after they saved the Baratie from an instrusion by Don Krieg and the Krieg Pirates, Sanji decided to join the Straw Hats in hopes of one day finding the All Blue, a legendary ocean that has fish from all four seas - a chef's paradise.

Tony Tony Chopper
  • Hometown: Drum Island
  • Crew Role: Doctor
  • Dream: To be able to cure anything

Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates and was the fifth member to join the crew. Chopper is a reindeer that ate the Human-Human Fruit, a Devil Fruit that allows its user to transform into a human hybrid at will. He was taught medicine by his two parental figures, Doctors Hiriluk and Kureha. He joined the Straw Hats after they saved Drum Island from it's evil former monarch Wapol and showed him the true meaning of friendship. Chopper aims to travel all across the world in hopes of one day being able to cure any disease.

Nico Robin
  • Hometown: Ohara
  • Crew Role: Archaeologist / Historian
  • Dream: To discover the world's true history

Nico Robin is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates and was the sixth member to join the crew. Robin was a child prodigy who grew up on the island of Ohara, which primarily consisted of archaeologists and historians. When she was eight years old, Ohara was destroyed by the World Government in an attempt to stop them from learning the true history of the world, but not before Robin was able to learn how to read Poneglyphs and be confirmed as a scholar. Twenty years later, Robin joined the Straw Hats in pursuit of the Rio Poneglyph which will hopefully tell that history.
